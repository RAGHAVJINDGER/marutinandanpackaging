About Us

Marutinandan Packaging is a leading corrugated box manufacturer based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Established in 2005, we specialize in manufacturing high-strength 3 ply, 5 ply, 7 ply and 9 ply corrugated boxes designed for industrial and commercial use.

We provide custom printed packaging solutions and unique box designs tailored to customer requirements. Our focus is durability, precision and timely delivery.

With modern machinery and strict quality control systems, we ensure every product meets high industry standards.

Our commitment to excellence makes us a trusted manufacturer and supplier for businesses across Ahmedabad and surrounding regions.
